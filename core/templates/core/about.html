About My Pet app

At the my pet app, we are dedicated to providing accurate and timely diagnostic services...

Brucellosis Diagnostics

We employ a combination of clinical assessment and advanced laboratory tests, including serology, PCR, and culture methods, to detect Brucella infection in humans and animals. Our procedures are designed with a strong emphasis on biosafety, safeguarding both our staff and the environment.

Mycobacteria Bovina Diagnosis

Our approach to diagnosing bovine tuberculosis in cattle involves comprehensive testing methods like culture, PCR, and tuberculin skin testing. This is crucial not only for animal health but also for controlling the spread of this disease to other animals and humans.

Leptospirosis Diagnosis

We utilize serological assays, PCR, and culture methods to detect Leptospira bacteria. Early diagnosis is essential for effective treatment and controlling the spread of leptospirosis in both humans and animals.

Rabies Diagnosis

Our laboratory conducts post-mortem examination of brain tissue samples in animals and employs tests like PCR and antibody detection in humans. This is critical for timely treatment and managing public health risks associated with rabies.

Foot-and-Mouth Disease Diagnosis

We use PCR, ELISA, and virus isolation from samples to diagnose foot-and-mouth disease. Timely and accurate diagnosis is vital for controlling outbreaks in livestock, thereby preventing significant economic losses in the agricultural sector.
